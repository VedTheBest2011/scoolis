campus life

kindergarten playground

was built in 1985

ISWK built the KG Playground because the kids in KG used to go to the playground which higher grade students were supposed to play. Lot of people complained about this, even the students, so ISWK managers built a playground for kids.

AC in classroom

was installed in 2006

The ISWK Team installed AC's in classrooms because they knew that air conditioning helps control the air quality of a classroom, so it's vital to not only have air conditioning in a classroom to filter the air but to make sure you replace your air conditioning filters frequently to ensure maximum performance of your unit.
